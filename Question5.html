My favorite potato recipe

Potato march

  1. Buy 2 Pounds of Potatoes
  2. Peel 2 Pounds of Potatoes
  3. Bring water to the boil
    1. Throw water in a pot.
    2. Put pot on cooker
    3. Switch cooker on
      1. Switch on the right plate
    4. Wait till it boils
  4. Boil them
  5. Mash them up when they are soft
